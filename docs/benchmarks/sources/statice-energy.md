# Statistics Iceland energy prices

## What it measures

Icelandic energy-price context, including published energy price tables for homes/industry and related market context.

## What it does not measure

It is not a station-level fuel retail observation and should not be merged into operator prices.

## Sample API call

```sh
curl 'https://px.hagstofa.is/pxen/api/v1/en/Umhverfi/Orka/'
```
