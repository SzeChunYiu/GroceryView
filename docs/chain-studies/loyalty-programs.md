## UI notes for `/account/loyalty`

- Model `costType` as `free`, `refundable_stake`, or `not_found_on_primary_source`; Coop needs `refundableStakeAmountSek: 100`.
- Store enrollment URLs separately from privacy/terms URLs so the UI can present “Join” and “Read data terms” as separate actions.
- Expose data-tradeoff badges: `identity_number`, `contact_details`, `purchase_history`, `digital_behavior`, `payment_identifier`, `partner_sharing`, `personalized_marketing`.
- Do not present Lidl Plus or City Gross PRIO as paid or free-without-conditions beyond what the primary pages say; the sources reviewed did not list a membership fee on the enrollment/terms pages.
