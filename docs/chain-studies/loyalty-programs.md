## Product guardrails for `/account/loyalty`

- Treat loyalty prices as account-bound eligibility, not public shelf evidence. GroceryView should only apply them after the shopper explicitly enables that chain or confirms membership.
- Show the non-monetary tradeoff next to each enrollment call-to-action: identity verification, purchase-history tracking, marketing/personalization, and possible partner/household linking.
- Keep costs explicit: Coop has a refundable 100 SEK member share; the other listed base memberships are free or have no separate public fee stated for the base loyalty enrollment.
- Do not ask for or store retailer passwords. Link out to the retailer enrollment page and store only the shopper's local preference that they hold that loyalty card.
- Re-check retailer terms before launch because loyalty conditions, bonus thresholds, and data-consent wording can change without GroceryView code changes.
