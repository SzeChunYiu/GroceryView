## Model-gap takeaways

- **Online coverage is uneven.** Apohem, Apoteket.se, Goodstore, Kronans, Mathem, Willys, and the pharmacy savings study have online price evidence; Lidl, Matpriskollen, Circle K, Shell, Tanka, and 10-11 should stay store/flyer/fuel/location scoped unless future sources expose online checkout prices.
- **Loyalty/member price modeling is required** across Willys, Goodstore, Apohem, Apoteket.se, Kronans, Lidl, Pressbyran, Circle K EXTRA, Shell/St1 app discounts, Tanka CarPay, and the broader Swedish loyalty-program chains. Matpriskollen mirrors source-chain membership-card requirements rather than owning the loyalty program.
- **Format variance is concrete for Willys and Matpriskollen.** Circle K, Pressbyran, and Shell have segment/status metadata but not grocery-style price tiers; most pharmacy and online-only studies found no format price levels.
- **Region pricing is mostly source-scoped rather than universal.** Matpriskollen preserves store-local offers; Lidl exposes regional offer maps; Willys, Circle K, Tanka, and N1 fuel rows need store/station context. Do not infer region prices for pharmacy or single-store studies without explicit rows.
- **Subscription pricing is currently positive only for Pressbyran magazine products.** Other loyalty programs are free or member/account based rather than subscription product prices.
- **App/coupon pricing needs explicit flags** for Goodstore, Apohem, Apoteket.se, Lidl, Matpriskollen, Pressbyran, Circle K EXTRA app rewards/charging, Shell/St1 app discounts, and loyalty-program flows. Kronans and Mathem document coupon mechanisms but only emit product coupon rows when concrete source rows require them.
- **Counter pricing remains a gap.** Goodstore mentions a cafe, Pressbyran mentions food/fika/workplace fridges, and several stores have service contexts, but no checked-in study verifies a same-item counter-versus-packaged price split.
