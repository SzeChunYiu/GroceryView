# GroceryView — Goal

Build a TradingView-style grocery price intelligence platform for Stockholm (expanding city-by-city).

**MVP goal**: Web + mobile app where users can track grocery prices, compare deals across supermarkets (ICA, Willys, Coop, Hemköp, Lidl), view price history, manage shopping lists with budget tracking, and get deal alerts.

**Current phase**: Research & architecture — workers should research competitor landscape, data sourcing options, tech stack, and produce initial architecture + feature specification documents.
